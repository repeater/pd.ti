Pioneer RESA Account

All endorsement candidates must create an account with Pioneer RESA at https://fcweb.pioneerresa.org/. Please complete your account at the beginning of the first course as all transcripts are created in this system. Without the account, we cannot process any transcripts nor can we enter you into the GaPSC database for endorsements.

Please register using your first and last name as they appear on your teaching certificate. Make sure to enter all information, including your teacher certificate number and date of birth, into the registration system. Failure to enter all information will create a delay in issuance of transcripts. Course transcripts, verification of enrollment and program recommendations to GaPSC will be made using this information.

PLEASE NOTE: The transcript will print directly from the information that you enter. If you misspell a word or type in all lower case or upper case, your transcript will reflect this. You may update the information in the registration system account whenever you have had a change in name, address, location, position, or email address. Please update rather than create a new account in the system.

Verification of Pioneer RESA as Your Endorsement Provider

You will receive an email from GaPSC during or shortly after the first course of the endorsement asking you to verify Pioneer RESA as your provider. You must complete this verification in order to receive your endorsement at the end of the program. Your Pioneer RESA student ID number is your teacher certification number. Should you not receive an email from PSC regarding verification, you may still go into your MyPSC account under Programs and verify.

If you cannot remember your certification number, you may go to http://www.gapsc.com/Certification/Lookup.aspx and enter your name. Your certificate number will be under your name and will say PSC account number.

Completion of Endorsement

All endorsement requirements must be completed within a two-year period from time of enrollment in the first course. Candidates who do not complete the program or who do not complete required assignments will not be awarded credit for the courses and/or endorsement.

Transcripts

Pioneer RESA will issue transcripts for each course upon notification from CaseNEX that all course requirements have been met. Please note: This process may take some time as instructors must grade all assignments and complete the appropriate spreadsheet, the spreadsheet must be sent to Pioneer RESA and transcripts processed. Transcripts are processed in the order in which they are received. Retain copies of transcripts for your records as you will send copies to GaPSC to add the endorsement to your certificate.

To avoid delays:

    Make sure you have created an account with Pioneer RESA and have entered all information including your teacher certificate number, mailing address and birthdate.
  • Make sure you have verified Pioneer RESA as your endorsement provider through your MyPSC account.
  • Make sure you respond to any email from Pioneer RESA requesting any missing information in a timely manner.

Adding the Endorsement to Your Certificate

When you have completed all courses for the endorsement, Pioneer RESA will send you your transcript for the last course along with instructions for adding the endorsement to your certificate. We will also electronically notify GaPSC that you have completed the program.

Once you receive the final transcript, you will complete a GaPSC application to add the endorsement to your certificate and send it along with your transcripts to GaPSC. Your HR Department will be able to assist you with the application.
