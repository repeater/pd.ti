A proven model to prepare educators for the real issues they face

Casenex PD follows an effective five-step approach
  1. Identify educational issues, problems, and opportunities.
  2. Recognize different perspectives and values that drive others' actions.
  3. Drawn up personal, theoretical, and empirical knowledge bases and apply them to the issue identified.
  4. Propose actions and potential solutions to the issue.
  5. Predict the possible outcomes of different actions.
